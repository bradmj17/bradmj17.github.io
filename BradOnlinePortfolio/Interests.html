Track and Field

I had played soccer for my entire life up until freshman year of high school. That year, I decided to try track and field. It turned out I was a great sprinter and I was quickly a member of my high school's varisty team. The following year, I left my soccer club and joined a track and field club based out of Boise called Idaho Dash Track Club.

The coaches fostered me into a great track and field athlete, and I ran the 100m, 200m, 400m, 4x100m, 4x200m, and the 4x400m; I was a sprinter. Unfortunately, some injuries and COVID-19 soured my opportunity to compete on the college level.
