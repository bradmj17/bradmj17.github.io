Frequently Asked Questions

What is GLOAM?

GLOAM and the GLOAMingo brand was created in 2013 to ensure that families and friends could float rivers and lakes in style. We sell water related products such as inflatables, sunscreen, towels, swimwear, and merchandise. Our goal as a team is to fulfill our customers and grow into a notable business.

Browse our catalog!

Do you ship internationally?

We are located in San Francisco, California. We will ship international although please allow for 30 business days for shipping overseas. US shipping can be expected to be delivered within 7 business days

What sizes do your products come in?

Both merchandise and inflatables range in sizes. From toddlers, to children, to adults - we've got it all.

How can I get a discount?

Navigating to the Contact Page will prompt you to sign up for the newsletter. Those who sign up for the newsletter can use their code for 15% off on their order of 50$ or more.

What is the GLOAMingo Newsletter?

Here we will send email newsletters and updates about upcoming products and sales. You get a free sticker for signing up and purchasing something within the first seven days.
