Bradley Johnson; About Me


Blurb

I'm Bradley Johnson. I am a twenty year-old software developer in Nampa, Idaho pursuing a programming career. Since graduating high school in 2020, I have been working tirelessly at College of Western Idaho to achieve my academic goals. In the spring of 2022 I enrolled in CWI's Software Development program after finishing my Idaho Education Certificate. The program's pacing is intense in order to prepare the students for employment and teaches several programming languages such as C#.

What Interests Me In Programming?

For some time, I wasn't interested in programming. Throughout all four years at high school, I took AutoCAD Drafting courses and was a top student in my class. However, in my senior year of high school, I took a college-level Programming course through Boise State University. In that class, I had a lot of fun and developed an entire mobile game single-handedly. I had discovered the joy of collaborating with other like-minded people to create a project that others will enjoy, and my objective is to pursue that feeling to a larger scale in terms of quality.
